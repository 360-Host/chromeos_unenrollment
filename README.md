This tool is used to redirect you outside of the chromeos sign in screen in an enrolled chromebook.
Steps:
1. When Chromebook says "Enterprise Enrollment", turn off the WIFI.
2. Then press CTRL + SHIFT + ALT + R.
3. Press "CANCEL."
4. Press enroll manually.
5. Input "google@d11.org" in the username slot and press "next."
6. Pass the bot test (IDK what it's called.)
7. Press "Sign-in options" at the bottom of the sign in screen.
8. Then click "Sign in with GitHub."
9. Scroll all the way down on the GitHub sign in screen and press "Docs."
10. Scroll all the way down again and press "Make a contribution."
11. On the top right search bar, search "chromeos_unenrollment."
12. Click on the repo you see currently.
13. And now you have access to all the URLs this page hosts!
